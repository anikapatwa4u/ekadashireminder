Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Ekadashi Reminder ("the app") is developed to help you keep track of Ekadashi fasting days and related reminders. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information the app does and does not handle.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. The app does not require an account, login, or any personal information to function.

Data Stored on Your Device

All information you create in the app — such as your selected location, custom reminders, and reminder preferences — is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not shared with us or any third party.

Notifications

The app uses local notifications to remind you of upcoming Ekadashi and related fasting days. These notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to any external service.

Calendar Access

With your permission, the app can add Ekadashi fasting days as events to your device's calendar so you never miss one. The app only writes these events at your request and does not read, collect, or transmit any of your existing calendar data. You can revoke calendar access at any time in your device settings.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact
