Frequently Asked Questions

How do reminders work?

The app schedules local notifications for upcoming Ekadashi and related fasting days based on your selected location. Make sure notifications are enabled for the app in your device settings.

How do I change my location?

Open the app and select your city from the location picker. Fasting times and break-fast (Paaran) windows are calculated for your chosen location.

How do I add events to my calendar?

When prompted, grant the app calendar access. You can then add Ekadashi fasting days to your device calendar directly from the app.

I'm not receiving notifications. What should I do?

Check that notifications are allowed for Ekadashi Reminder in your device's Settings, and that Do Not Disturb / Focus modes are not blocking them.
